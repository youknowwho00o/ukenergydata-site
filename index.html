AUTOMATED CONTENT

Daily energy price reports

This site is designed to publish one short, structured report per day:

  • Latest Ofgem cap levels & effective periods
  • Selected supplier tariffs & Agile-style dynamic prices
  • Day-on-day change indicators and simple commentary
  • Exportable tables for analysts & journalists

Next step (we will add): a script that fetches data, generates a Markdown/HTML report, and commits it automatically via GitHub Actions.

ABOUT

What is ukenergydata.co.uk?

A small, automated project focused on turning raw UK energy data into clear, daily-updated signals: caps, tariffs, and trends.

The goal is to run as a fully scripted “information machine”: collect → process → publish, with minimal manual work.
